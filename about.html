Why We Built This

The gap between what enterprise software can do and what small businesses actually get

The best business software runs inside large companies. Small businesses get Tally, Excel, and a WhatsApp group. We set out to close that gap.

We have worked inside organisations that run high-end business systems — payroll engines processing tens of thousands of employees, accounting software reconciling hundreds of accounts a day, compliance tools that talk directly to government portals. We have seen what those systems can do.

We have also seen what they cost: large licence fees, months of implementation, specialist consultants, and then more fees every year. All of it priced for companies that can absorb it. A construction firm with 40 workers or a school managing 600 students never gets access to this quality of software — not at any price they can actually afford.

We believe that is wrong. The work a construction company does is real. The books a school needs to keep are real. The GST a coaching centre has to file is real. The software that helps them should be real too — complete, reliable, and priced for the actual world.

So we built Munimo: the software your munim (your trusted bookkeeper) would have built if they were a software engineer. Understands your industry. Works on day one. Priced to what your business can invest.

The Team

Enterprise software experience. Startup honesty.

Munimo is built by software professionals who have spent years designing and implementing business systems for large organisations across manufacturing, financial services, and enterprise IT. We know what good business software looks like from the inside — and what it takes to make it reliable.

Our expertise spans financial accounting engines, GST and tax compliance automation, workforce management, and cloud infrastructure security. Every feature in Munimo has been shaped by one question: will a busy business owner who is not a software person actually find this useful?

We are based in India. We understand rupees, GST, BOCW registers, TDS challans, and the language your accountant speaks. Munimo was not translated from a Western product. It was built from scratch, for Indian businesses, Indian regulations, and the pace of work in Indian markets.
