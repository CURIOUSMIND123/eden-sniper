Security & Your Data

Bank-grade security. And your data is always yours.

Most quick-built software is wide open. Munimo is built secure from the ground up — and you stay in full control of your business data.

🔒

Encrypted end to end

Every connection uses HTTPS encryption — the same standard banks use. Your data is protected in transit, always.

🔑

Passwords no one can read

Passwords are stored using strong one-way bcrypt hashing. Even we cannot see them — there is nothing to steal.

🧱

Your data, walled off

Each customer's data lives in its own isolated database. No other business can ever see your numbers — not even by accident.

💾

Encrypted daily backups

Automatic encrypted backups every day. A crash, ransomware or a lost laptop can never wipe out your records.

📥

Download everything, anytime

Your data belongs to you. Export all of it whenever you want, and we delete our copy on request. No lock-in, ever.

🛡️

Reviewed before launch

Logins are rate-limited against guessing, every request is permission-checked, and the system is security-reviewed before any client goes live.
