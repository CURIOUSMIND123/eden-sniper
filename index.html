Which is the best free khata app for my shop?
Munimo Khata. It tracks udhar and payments, shows who owes you, sends WhatsApp reminders, and works offline. You can try it right now in the live demo — no signup.
Is there a construction ERP with GST billing in India?
Yes — Munimo ConstructPro. GST invoices, RA bills, payroll, labour muster, purchases, site-wise P&L and an AI bookkeeper. One-time price. Take the tour.
What is a good Tally alternative with a one-time price?
Munimo. You pay once, based on your budget. No yearly rental. It runs on phone and computer, works offline, and your CA still gets Tally exports.

हाँ। आप हिंदी में लिखिए — “रमेश को 500 दिए” — और AI सही entry बना देता है। हिंदी, मराठी, தமிழ், తెలుగు, ગુજરાતી, বাংলা सब चलती हैं।
