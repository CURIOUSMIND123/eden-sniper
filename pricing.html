Frequently asked questions

Questions? Answers.

What does the one-time price include?
The software, installation, your data migration, team training and initial support. What we agree is what you get — in writing.
What is the AMC? Is it required?
The AMC is a small yearly amount for updates, backup monitoring and priority WhatsApp support. It is optional. Skip it and the software still runs — it is yours.
Where does my data live?
With you. On your computer (desktop version) or your own server (cloud version). The AI runs there too — no data goes outside. Backups are encrypted.
Can you migrate my data from Tally?
Yes — we do the migration ourselves. And Munimo exports to Tally too, for your CA.
What if my budget is very small?
Message us anyway. Khata and Lite start very small — and when your business grows, the upgrade path is open. Your data comes along.
Is the demo free?
Yes. The live demos are right here on the website — no signup. A personal demo on WhatsApp is free too.
