Sawaal? Jawab.

One-time price me kya-kya aata hai?
Software, installation, aapka data migration, team training aur shuru ka support. Jo tay hoga, wahi milega — likh ke.
AMC kya hai? Zaroori hai?
AMC ek chhota yearly amount hai — updates, backup monitoring aur priority WhatsApp support ke liye. Optional hai. Nahi loge to bhi software chalta rahega, aapka hai.
Data kahan rehta hai?
Aapke paas. Desktop version me aapke computer par, cloud version me aapke apne server par. AI bhi wahi chalta hai — koi data bahar nahi jaata. Backup encrypted hote hain.
Tally se data aa sakta hai?
Haan — hum migration khud karte hain. Aur Munimo se Tally export bhi hai, aapke CA ke liye.
Budget bahut chhota hai to?
Phir bhi WhatsApp karo. Khata aur Lite bahut kam me shuru ho jaate hain — aur jab business bade, upgrade ka rasta khula hai. Data wahi rahega.
Demo ka charge?
Zero. Live demos website par hi hain — bina signup ke. Personal demo WhatsApp par book karo, woh bhi free.
