The Problem

Still running your business on Tally + Excel + WhatsApp?

Most construction companies juggle three or four disconnected tools. The books never match what is happening on site — and month-end takes days instead of hours.

📒

Scattered records

Site expenses in a diary, labour attendance in a notebook, invoices in Excel. Putting it all together at month-end wastes 2–3 full days.

🧾

GST stress every month

Collecting data for your CA, fixing mismatches, chasing missing invoices. One filing mistake can cost up to ₹10,000 in penalties.

👷

Labour wages by hand

Daily muster rolls, overtime, advances, PF and ESIC — calculated manually for dozens of workers. Errors lead to disputes on site.

🏗️

No site-wise profit view

Four sites running, but nobody knows which one is making money and which one is bleeding. You find out only when the project ends.

💸

Cash flow guesswork

Who has paid, who hasn't, what is due to vendors this week — answering a simple question takes hours of digging.

🤝

Subcontractor disputes

RA bills, work measurements, advances against work — tracked on paper, argued over later. No clean record means no quick resolution.

Labour & Workforce

Muster rolls and wages — fully automatic

Mark daily attendance for every gang on every site. Weekly and monthly wages calculate themselves, including overtime and advance recovery. Print BOCW-compliant wage registers any time an inspector asks.

Compliance

GST returns your CA will love

GSTR-1 is generated automatically from your invoices. Export the JSON file and upload it straight to the GSTN portal — no retyping, no mismatches. TDS challans tracked in their own register.

  • GSTR-1 JSON in the exact GSTN portal format
  • TDS challan register — 194C, 194J, 194I and more
  • Input tax credit visibility per site
  • Trial Balance & Balance Sheet, CA-ready
Money Control

Bank reconciliation that does the work for you

Import your bank statement CSV and the software matches transactions automatically. Multiple bank accounts, every receipt and payment recorded, one combined cash position.

  • Auto-match imported statement lines
  • Multiple bank accounts, one view
  • Transfers, receipts, payments — full ledger
  • Spot missing or duplicate entries instantly
Intelligence
